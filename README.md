### 🌐 Overview
Gaptical is an independent initiative dedicated to technological sovereignty and structural innovation. We are redefining the digital stack from the ground up, focusing on the seamless integration between hardware and software ecosystems.

### 🏛 The Pillars
*   **Fundamental Architecture:** Building from-scratch systems designed for performance and security.
*   **Vertical Integration:** Ensuring every layer, from logic to interface, operates in perfect synergy.
*   **Technological Sovereignty:** Empowering users through open-source infrastructure and transparent design.

### 👥 Organization Hierarchy
Our operations are governed by a multi-level internal structure to ensure the integrity of the vision:
*   **L0: Global Directorate** – Strategic governance and vision.
*   **L1: Technical Oversight** – Standards and cross-project synergy.
*   **L2: Systems & Environments** – Core development and implementation.
*   **L2: Design & Standards** – Visual identity and interaction protocols.
*   **L3: Platform Operations** – Ecosystem scaling and global infrastructure.
